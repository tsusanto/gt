Quick and easy comfort food.

Prepare sauce in a bowl with the following:
	* 1.5 tablespoons korean chili powder
	* 2 tablespoons of gochuchang
	* 1 tablespoon soy sauce
	* 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
	* 1 tablespoon sugar
	* 2 tablespoon mirin
